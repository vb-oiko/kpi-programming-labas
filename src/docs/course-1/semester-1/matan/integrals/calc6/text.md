$$\frac{x^3+6x^2+13x+8}{x(x+2)^3} = \frac{A}{x} + \frac{B}{x+2} + \frac{C}{(x+2)^2} + \frac{D}{(x+2)^3}
$$

$$

    12A  +4B  +2C  +D  =   13,  \\

$$
